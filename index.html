About Us

We offer coaching for the prestigious Civil Service Examination. We are based out of the great southern city of Tamil Nadu, Nagercoil. Since inception, we have been helping our students obtaining ranks in services like IAS, IPS, IRS, TNPSC. We are proud to say we are the best TNSPC and bank exam coaching center in Nagercoil.

We provide excellent training in gaining conceptual knowledge in various subjects viz. General Studies, Sociology, Geography, Public Administration, History, Political Science, Tamil Literature and Psychology.

The faculty members are highly qualified and are from reputed institutions. We specialise approaching the subjects from the examination point of view, supplementing it with impeccable notes, periodical exams and in-depth review of answers
